### Enhancement 5.1: Role-Based Access Control

**What it is:** Different permissions for different team members

**For Non-Technical Users:**
> Like keys to different rooms in a building. Some people can enter all rooms (admins), some can enter certain rooms (editors), and some can only look through windows (viewers).

### Enhancement 5.2: Audit Trail

**What it is:** Complete history of who did what and when

**For Non-Technical Users:**
> Like security camera footage. If something goes wrong, you can see exactly who made what change and when.

### Enhancement 5.3: Comments & Annotations

**What it is:** Add notes and discussions to resources

**For Non-Technical Users:**
> Like sticky notes on a whiteboard. Team members can leave notes like "This server is being decommissioned next month" or "Contact John before making changes."
